📝 How to format your workout routines for import

## Week 1 – Foundation - Morning walk 20 min [ ] - Evening walk 6-8 min [ ] - Ankle pumps 50 [ ] !video(https://youtu.be/abc123) - Heel slides 20 [x] - Strength: squats 8, bridges 15 [ ]

Rules:

  • ## Title = creates a section heading
  • - task [ ] = creates a checkbox item (empty)
  • - task [x] = creates a checkbox item (pre‑checked)
  • !video(URL) = adds a clickable video link
  • Any exercise name not in your inventory is automatically added
