📝 How to format your workout routines for import

## Week 1-2 – Foundation - Morning walk 20 min [ ] - Ankle pumps 50 [ ] !video(https://youtu.be/abc123) - Strength session: squats 8, bridges 15 [ ] (3x per week) - Evening walk 6-8 min [ ] ## Week 3-4 – Increase - Evening walk 8-10 min [ ] - Strength session: squats 10, bridges 18 [ ] (3x per week)

Rules:

  • ## Week X-Y – defines a period. Tasks inside will only appear for dates whose week number falls between X and Y (based on the import date).
  • - task [ ] – daily checkbox.
  • - task [ ] (Nx per week) – weekly frequency task. The app counts how many times you check it in the current week (Monday–Sunday) and shows a counter.
  • !video(URL) – adds a video link.
  • Unknown exercises are auto-added to inventory.

💬 For AI / ChatGPT prompts:
"Create a 8-week workout program for my Achilles tendon recovery. Use format with ## Week X-Y headers, - items, [ ] checkboxes, and for tasks that should be done multiple times per week write (Nx per week) after the checkbox. Include !video(URL) for stretches."
